The Colosseum

-- Rome

The Colosseum is the biggest attraction of modern-day Rome.

Machu Picchu

-- Peru

The ancient Inca city of Machu Picchu is arguably the most impressive ruined city in the world.

Borobudur

-- Indonesia

Set in a steamy jungle with three volcanoes providing the backdrop, Borobudur is Indonesia's top tourist attraction.

Mount Fuji

-- Japan

Mount Fuji is one of three Holy Mountains, all of which are UNESCO World Heritage Sites.

Central Park

-- New York City

Central Park is one of the most famous public spaces in the world.
